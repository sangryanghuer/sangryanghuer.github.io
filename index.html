What is Samsung GSAP?

Samsung GSAP is Samsung Global Startup Acceleration Program. This program is conducted as a part of Corporate Social Responsibility from Samsung Electronics.

Vision for GSAP

We believe in your potential and we will help you.

Communication

We always have an open mind and a compassionate heart to understand startups’ ideas and dreams.

Change

With our expertise and experience we try to help startups' business to be more valuable.

Confidence

We hope this Boot Camp could build up and increase startups' self-confidence.
